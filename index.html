This course provides a comprehensive framework for understanding how strategic leadership drives sustainable entrepreneurship. It integrates innovation, ethics, and organizational change to align business strategies with global sustainability standards and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

  • To apply design thinking and change management in sustainability-oriented enterprises.
  • To evaluate and construct sustainable business models aligned with SDGs.

ðŸ“š Module-Wise Structure

Module 1: Leadership in the Sustainability Era (12 Hours)

Strategic Leadership Frameworks, Challenges, Sustainable Entrepreneurship Drivers, Business Model Types, Visionary Thinking, Organizational Culture, Collaborative Leadership, Decision-Making, Risk Management, and Climate Change Implications.

Module 2: Sustainability in Business Models (12 Hours)

Triple Bottom Line (Profit, People, Planet), Metrics and Measurement, Circular Economy, Designing for Sustainability, Stakeholder Collaboration, and Technological Innovations for Business Sustainability.

Module 3: Leadership Skills for Sustainability (12 Hours)

Visionary and Transformational Leadership, Ethical Frameworks, Team Leadership Dynamics, Ethical Dilemmas, and High-Performance Sustainable Teams.

Module 4: Innovation and Change Management (12 Hours)

Sustainable Innovation Strategies, Change Management Models, Systems Thinking, Design Thinking, Lean Startup Methods, and Evaluating the Impact of Change Initiatives.

Module 5: Global Frameworks and Trends (12 Hours)

UN SDGs, Paris Agreement, ESG Policies, Role of AI, Blockchain, and IoT in Sustainability, Consumer Behavior, Green Financing, Globalization Trends, and Capstone Project on Sustainable Entrepreneurship.
